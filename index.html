About Me

My Journey So Far

Let's start with my name, 'Mansi' rooted in the Sanskrit word 'मनस्' (manas) representing the harmony of heart and mind, symbolizing creativity, wisdom and thoughtfulness, like the energy I bring to my work ⚡

My journey began with love for art, nurtured by my mom and led me to a Bachelor's in Textiles and Apparel Design. Over time, my curiosity for digital design sparked and that's when I found my true calling — Product Design 👾. Now, as a Master's student in Human-Centered Design, I know this is where I'm meant to be ❤️

Design isn't just what I do, it's who I am. I can't wait to see where it takes me next! 🚀

What I Do For Fun?

When I'm not immersed in design, you'll find me staying active whether it's fitness 🏋️, reading 📖, or exploring places 🗺️. I also love diving into creative adventures like pottery and trekking. On quiet days, I reconnect with my roots through embroidery 🪡, illustrating or painting 🎨

Got something on your mind? Let's chat 💬 over a cup of chai ☕ and dive into fun, meaningful conversations!

Life's a journey, and I'm all in — learning and growing with every step! ✨
